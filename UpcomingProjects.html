Highlights

  • The Land Parcel is located in a predominantly residential estate within the Tengah Town Planning Area, bounded by PIE and Bukit Batok Road. This new development is adjacent to another EC project and near the upcoming JE2 Tengah Park MRT as part of the Jurong Regional Line.
  • Residents will be close to educational institutions such as ACS Primary School, Kranji Primary School, Jurong Primary School, Yuhua Primary School, Princess Elizabeth Primary School, Crest Secondary School, Bukit Batok Secondary School, Jurongville Secondary School, River Valley High, Dulwich College, Jurong JC, Jurong Pioneer JC, Nanyang Technological University of Singapore, etc.
  • The development of JLD will revolve around 4 themes - Hub for Future Economy, New Paradigm of Mobility, Smart and Sustainable District and District of Gardens and Waters. Residents of Plantation Close will equally enjoy the benefits of these transformations.
  • The Land Parcel is conveniently linked to Pan-Island Expressway (PIE), and Bukit Batok Road, providing easy access to the Jurong Lake District, Jurong Innovation Distric and the Tuas Port.
  • The number of Dwelling Units (DUs) for the proposed development shall not exceed 560. Maximum building height is 70.0 m
